1. Team Exercise

Background

Apple is a prime example of a company whose success is built on innovation. It has had many successful innovations throughout its history that have spurred growth and strengthened customer loyalty. Through its unique and cutting edge product offerings, Apple has demonstrated all the types of innovation that we have discussed today.

Guidance

Use the excerpt provided and do your own outside research to learn more about strategy and innovation at Apple, focusing specifically on the Apple iPad. Craft a memo addressing the following questions:

  • How did Apple's strategy pivot after Steve Jobs returned to his role as CEO in 1997? What are the implicit tradeoffs that Apple has made in carving out a strategic position?
  • Is the iPad consistent with Apple's strategy? How?
  • Is the iPad a sustaining or disruptive innovation? Be sure to explain your answer.
  • Would you describe the iPad as a successful innovation?
  • What are the major risk factors that Apple faces going forward? Think carefully about Apple's strategy and history of innovation.

For each of the questions above, be sure to explain your answers using relevant, factual information (both qualitative and quantitative).
