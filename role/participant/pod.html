What Is Your Type?

The MBTI Assessment: TypeCoach Verifier

You on Your Team

Throughout the Fullbridge Program, you will be asked to work as a member of a team. As you'll see firsthand, effective teamwork allows for the development of concepts and deliverables that could not have been created by a single individual.

True teamwork requires significant effort and awareness on everyone's part. The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator, or MBTI, is one tool that many companies and individuals use to increase self-awareness. The MBTI offers useful information about how people perceive the world, absorb information, and make decisions.

You will now take the MBTI assessment online, using an interactive tool called the TypeCoach Verifier. Please follow the instructions in the column to the right.

MBTI Assessment

Your MBTI Verifier results will provide you with insight into your personal style preferences, how those preferences influence your approach to information and interactions, and how other people might experience working with you. This assessment also provides a framework for thinking about the preferred styles of others. There are no correct or incorrect answers or good or bad "scores," and this assessment does not measure abilities or intelligence. It contains no value judgments about preferred styles, nor is it a psychological test that can reveal private aspects of your personality.

Instructions
  1. Click Here to Launch the Assessment.
  2. Follow the on-screen prompts.
    1. Enter your email address
    2. You may select your own password
    3. Enter the promo code fullbridge
  3. Complete the assessment (approx. 20 minutes).
  4. Download, save, and review your results.

You can return to your assessment at any time by following the original link and entering your email and password. If you need to stop mid-assessment, returning to the site later will allow you to resume where you left off.

Using the MBTI

Whether you have taken the MBTI before or are taking it for the first time, in this Program, you will use the results in a unique way that will help you be a strong, contributing team member while improving your own self-awareness.
